## Root Cause Analysis

The overwhelming majority of findings (28 of 42) trace to a single root cause: **the patch catalog data pipeline is ingesting raw feed data but not performing enrichment**. No CVE correlation, no OS family tagging, no endpoint matching, no status lifecycle, no category rendering in list view, no proper patch naming.

| Root Cause Group | Findings | Severity Range |
|-----------------|----------|---------------|
| catalog-data-pipeline | 10 | 5 critical, 5 major |
| ui-design | 15 | 0 critical, 5 major, 8 minor, 2 nitpick |
| accessibility | 5 | 0 critical, 3 major, 2 minor |
| detail-page-bugs | 3 | 0 critical, 3 major |
| data-integrity-bug | 2 | 1 critical, 1 major |
| missing-feature | 3 | 0 critical, 3 major |
| filter-state-management | 1 | 0 critical, 1 major |
| list-detail-inconsistency | 1 | 1 critical |

**Fix priority: catalog-data-pipeline first (eliminates 10 findings including 5 critical), then ui-design (15 findings), then accessibility (5 findings).**
